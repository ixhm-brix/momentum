    About

    This ledger is kept by F. Ishimwe, a student who believes that small spendings deserve careful handwriting.

    Momentum — because small spendings, faithfully recorded, gather speed — is a student finance tracker. Log what you spend, search it with regular expressions, watch the week's trend, and let a firm red stamp tell you when the budget is gone. Built with vanilla HTML, CSS and JavaScript. No frameworks; nothing between you and the page.

    — F. Ishimwe
